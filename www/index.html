OnlineTA for Advanced Programming 2016

Hello, AP student!

This is the site for our online testing. Use this to test your assignments before submitting them to Absalon. The tests are not a complete substitute for human correction, but they should give an indication of how good your solution is.

We strongly encourage you to test that your submission meets our format and interface requirements.

Assignment 1: A SubScript Interpreter

For assignment 1, run this in your working directory: 

    curl -F src=@src.zip -F report=@report.pdf -F group=@group.txt https://ap16.onlineta.org/grade/1

You can also try out our Haskell script: OnlineTA.hs.

Usage:

    ./OnlineTA[.hs] <assignment-identifier> <workdir>

For instance, for assignment 1, you might do something like this: 

    ./OnlineTA.hs 1 ~/ap16/assignments/1

The identifier for assignment 1 is 1.

When you are ready, submit assignment 1 on Absalon.
